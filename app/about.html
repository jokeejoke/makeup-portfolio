About me

I’m a licensed esthetician and makeup artist based in New York. I’ve been in the beauty industry
for 4 years.

Here’s a bit more about my passion for beauty and skincare. Great makeup starts with great skin. I love helping people feel more confident with their skin. I perform customized facial treatments and laser hair removal.

I also specialize in makeup. My artistic philosophy had always been to find the beauty of each individual rather than follow a trend. While my signature style is fresh and natural, I am able to create any look you desire.

I look forward to meeting you! I will help you look and feel your best!
